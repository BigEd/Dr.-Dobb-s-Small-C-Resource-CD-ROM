Welcome to New Micros, Inc.

Your source for embedded controllers

New Micros, Inc. (NMI) produces small, dedicated, board-level computer products. Various Single Board Computers (SBCs) are based on a number of different microprocessors (CPUs). Added peripherals, from serial ports to servo motor controllers, and Analog to Digital Converters (A/D) to Optoisolated Input/Outputs, expand the SBCs capabilities. The SBCs and peripherals can stack together to make compact units, depending on the mechanical format, used.

Our packaged development systems include a computer and programming languages. NMI provides software for program development, and embedded applications. Small C compilers, Max-FORTH, and BASIC are examples of development languages. A new real-time programming language, ISO-MAX, provides Iso-structure (mulittasking without a multitasker!) and a graphical programming interface (GUI). User manuals, some assemblers and utilities, and other software, are also available.When a multiboard solution won't do, or additional features are desired on an existing product, we make custom circuit boards to your specification.

Our real-time programming system, IsoMAX, promises to revolutionize the way real-time programming applications are done, using an Iso-structure layer to implement multitasking without a multitasker. Combining hardened, optoisolated, inputs and outputs with a motherboard, the NMIO, series boards are larger, more "industrially" oriented than previouslly offered by NMI. Available in 16 or 32 Inputs/Outputs combinations. Visit the News Page at our web site at http://www.newmicros.com for details on IsoMAX and NMIO, plus Easy-A MultiDrop Communications Protocol, and MAXDDE and other items of interest.

For 15 years we've been making circuit boards of our own design, and designing them for others.
